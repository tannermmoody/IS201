The BYU Cougars are a collegiate football team representing Brigham Young University. They are the best team in college football, in my opinion of course. BYU has a rich football history, with multiple conference championships and a national championship in 1984. The team has produced numerous NFL players and is known for its strong passing offense and disciplined play style. BYU plays its home games at LaVell Edwards Stadium in Provo, Utah, which has a seating capacity of over 63,000 fans. It is one of the most exciting places to watch a college football game!
